Chicken Fajitas Recipe

A delicious and easy-to-make Mexican dish.

Prep Time:

20 minutes

Cook Time:

15-20 minutes

Servings:

4-6

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into thin strips
  • 1/2 cup lime juice
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 large bell peppers (any color), sliced
  • 8 small flour tortillas
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional toppings: avocado, sour cream, shredded cheese, cilantro, salsa

Instructions:

  1. In a large resealable plastic bag, combine lime juice, olive oil, garlic, oregano, cumin, and cayenne pepper (if using).
  2. Add the chicken to the marinade, turning to coat. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes or up to 2 hours.
  3. Preheat a large skillet or griddle over medium-high heat.
  4. Remove the chicken from the marinade, letting any excess liquid drip off.
  5. Add the chicken to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes.
  6. Transfer the chicken to a plate and cover with foil to keep warm.
  7. In the same skillet, add the sliced onion and bell peppers. Cook until they are tender and lightly charred, about 5-7 minutes.
  8. Warm the tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds.
  9. Assemble the fajitas by slicing the cooked chicken and vegetables into thin strips. Serve with warm tortillas, and add your desired toppings.
